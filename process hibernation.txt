Process "Hibernate"
 - Event is sent to process to tell it it is being put into hibernation mode.
 - Process can request up to 1 second to prepare for hibernation
 - Events will no longer be sent to the process until it is brought out of hibernation mode.
 - Scheduler will not allocate time for the process
 - Process will be sent an event to come out of hibernation
 - If the process is quit while in hibernation, it is brought out of hibernation before being closed
